# Customer Churn Prediction

 ## Objective
 Predict customer churn using machine learning.

 ## Tools
 Python, Scikit-learn, Pandas, Matplotlib

 ## Model
 Logistic Regression / Random Forest

 ## Results
 - Accuracy: 82%
 - Key variables impacting churn

 ## Insights
The model demonstrates strong performance in identifying non-churn customers and a reasonable ability to detect churn cases. However, the presence of false negatives indicates that some at-risk customers are not being captured, highlighting an opportunity to improve recall.

Feature importance analysis reveals that tenure, monthly charges, and total charges are the primary drivers of churn, suggesting that both customer lifecycle stage and pricing play a critical role in customer retention.

From a business perspective, these insights indicate that companies should focus on improving early customer engagement, optimizing pricing strategies, and enhancing the payment experience to reduce churn. Additionally, prioritizing high-risk customers based on predicted probabilities can help allocate retention resources more effectively.
